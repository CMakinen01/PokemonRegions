Hoenn is the third region to be introduced in the Pokemon series. 

Hoenn is mainly known for its' starter Pokemon, being Torchic, Mudkip, and Treecko, as well as the Legendary Pokemon Rayquaza.

Hoenn also intoduced Double Battles.

The Champion in this region is Stephen or Wallace, depending on the version you purchase.

Hoenn also has a "superboss" in its' Emerald version, being Stephen.
