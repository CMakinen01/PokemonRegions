Johto is the second region to be introduced in the Pokemon series. 

Johto is mainly known for its' starter Pokemon, being Charmander, Squirtle, and Bulbasaur, as well as the Legendary Pokemon Ho-oh and Lugia.

Johto intoduced the Dark/Steel Types, as well as held items.

The Champion in this region is Lance, a previous Elite 4 member.

Johto also has a "superboss" being Red from the Kanto region.
