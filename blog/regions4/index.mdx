Sinnoh is the fourth region to be introduced in the Pokemon series. 

Sinnoh is mainly known for its' starter Pokemon, being Chimchar, Piplup, and Turtwig, as well as the Legendary Pokemon Dialga, Palkia, and Giratina.

The Champion in this region is Cynthia, arguably one of the hardest Champion battles in the series.
