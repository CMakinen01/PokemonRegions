Hisui is the ninth region to be introduced in the Pokemon series. 

Hisui is mainly known for its' starter Pokemon, being Cyndaquil, Oshawott, and Rowlet, with their regional evolutions distinguishing them from their original forms.

Hisui is the only Pokemon game to have in battle player movement, as well as player vs Pokemon boss fights.

There is no Champion in this region, but there is a final boss, being Volo. This battle also is the only battle where the opponent uses 8 Pokemon as opposed to the maximum of 6.
