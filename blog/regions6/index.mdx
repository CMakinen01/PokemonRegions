Kalos is the sixth region to be introduced in the Pokemon series. 

Kalos is mainly known for its' starter Pokemon, being Fennekin, Froakie, and Chespin, as well as the Legendary Pokemon Xerneas and Yveltal.

Kalos also introduced Mega Evolution, the first mid battle powerup mechanics.

The Champion in this region is Diantha.
