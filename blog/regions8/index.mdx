Galar is the eighth region to be introduced in the Pokemon series. 

Galar is mainly known for its' starter Pokemon, being Scorbunny, Sobble, and Grookey, as well as the Legendary Pokemon Zacian and Zamazenta.

Galar also introduced Dynamaxing, the third mid battle powerup mechanics.

The Champion in this region is Leon.
