Alola is the seventh region to be introduced in the Pokemon series. 

Alola is mainly known for its' starter Pokemon, being Litten, Popplio, and Rowlet, as well as the Legendary Pokemon Solgaleo and Lunala.

Alola also introduced Z-Moves, a powerful once a battle move

The Champion in this region is the professor, Kukui.
