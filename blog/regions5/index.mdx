Unova is the fifth region to be introduced in the Pokemon series. 

Unova is mainly known for its' starter Pokemon, being Tepig, Oshawott, and Snivy, as well as the Legendary Pokemon Reshiram and Zekrom.

Unova also introduced Triple Battles.

The Champion in this region is Iris.
